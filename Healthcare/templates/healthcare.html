BIOTECHNOLOGY

Biotechnology is the application of science and technology to develop products and processes for improving human life. It combines biology, chemistry, and engineering to create solutions in healthcare, agriculture, and industry. From developing vaccines to creating genetically modified crops, biotechnology has revolutionized many fields. It plays a crucial role in addressing global challenges like food security and disease control. Digital Payment Image

LIFE SCIENCE

Life Science is the study of living organisms, including their structure, function, growth, and evolution. It explores the complexities of life, from microscopic cells to entire ecosystems. This field encompasses various disciplines like biology, zoology, and genetics, contributing to advancements in healthcare, agriculture, and environmental conservation.
